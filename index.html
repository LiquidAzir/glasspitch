How to Play

Move

← ↑ → ↓Swipe to steer the highlighted player. They keep running that way, so you only steer.

Pinch (tap)

pinchOne button, context aware. The chip at the bottom always shows what it will do:
  • PASS — slides the ball to the best teammate ahead of you.
  • SHOOT — when you reach shooting range, pinch shoots at goal. Steer first to aim the corner.
  • TACKLE — when defending and near the ball, pinch to win it back.

The rest is automatic

You always control the player nearest the action — control switches for you. Teammates make runs, mark up, and your keeper saves on its own.

Pause

↑↓↑↓Swipe up-down-up-down to pause (or Esc on desktop).
